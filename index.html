Welcome to Project Riley

"At Project Riley, our motto is, “We Don’t Want Your Money, We Want YOU!”. We want you to experience, first hand, the joy and fulfillment that comes from working directly with the people, animals, and the organizations you support and for you to understand how valuable a little time and effort really is.

Volunteer Your Time

Project Riley was created on the belief that time is more valuable than money and that volunteering one’s time and attention is one of the most selfless acts a person can do. When people donate their time, not only are they supporting an important cause, they are investing themselves, their unique abilities, and their passion into that cause, giving it the power it needs to succeed.

Make An Impact

At Project Riley, we know that sometimes it takes the smallest amount of effort to make a huge impact in someone’s life. That’s why we encourage people to give as little or as much time as they can to volunteer in the community and participate in the events offered. We make a point to look for opportunities that incorporate personal interaction and that encourage volunteers to make a connection with those in need.

Inspire Change

Project Riley is more than just a volunteer organization. One of our primary goals is to inspire positive and progressive change in the community. We want the people we help and the people who volunteer with us to feel empowered and understand that they have the ability to initiate change in all areas of their life and in this world. What type of change we will inspire will vary from person to person because we understand that everyone is motivated to take action for different reasons.

Looking for a volunteer event. Check out our event page!

We want people to fully understand why their presence and choice to volunteer is so important. Whether it’s a large single event or a series of small, reoccurring actions, it all contributes to building strong, reliable, altruistic support for people and animals that need the time and attention of their fellow community members.

We can help people tap into what motivates them by providing the opportunities to see and experience the various needs of our society. This will allow them identify the ways they can support the efforts in place that will lead to positive change for the causes they feel most passionate about.

Volunteerism is also a way to bring together families, friends, and communities and allows us to experience diversity, learn empathy, develop skills, and connect with people in ways you normally couldn’t if you are only sending a check.
